About me

My hobby

I loved art and all beautiful things since I was a child. After that, I studied at an art school in China and worked in interior design for more than 10 years after graduation. I also enjoy hiking, music and movies. My favorite music is you raise me up, and one of my favorite movies is Titanic 1997.

My goal

I am a lifelong learner.Realizing my own lack of knowledge, I gave up my comfortable life in China and came to Canada to start my education again.I want to challenge myself to go further and further on the road of design.

My work

Career

I engaged in soft decoration design for 17 years. I specialize in soft decoration design for five-star hotels in Shanghai,China.The projects involve domestic and overseas projects. I have long-term cooperation with hotel groups such as Peninsula, Hilton, InterContinental, and Mandarin. The work content includes material selection. Matching all hotel areas (lobby, lobby bar, All Day Dining, Chinese restaurant, SPA, specialty restaurants, Bar, guest rooms, etc.) involves furniture, lighting, materials, sanitary ware, art and accessories, etc.Settled in Ningbo in 2017, specializing in the formulation and implementation of soft decoration plans for sales offices and model houses. We have close cooperation with real estate groups such as Greentown, Fubon, China Resources, Youngor, Wannian Jiye, Powerlong, and Midea.
